Rules

Stats

Attack - Affects how much damage the card can deal
Defense - Affects how much damage the card can take
HP - How much damage the card can take before being defeated

Moves

Fast Attack - Normally deals damage based on the attacker’s attack minus the defender’s defense
Slow attack - Normally deals damage equal to the attacker’s attack, ignoring the defender’s defense
Defend - Double’s the user’s defense for the turn. If the opponent uses a fast attack that would deal 0 damage after calculation, 3 damage is reflected to the attacker
Anticipate - Counters any slow attack and refects the damage back at the attacker, but gains 0 defense against fast attacks
Abilities - Varies based on card
Switch - Switch the current active card out for the selected card
